Datasets Similar to the Anthropic Economic Index
If you are looking for datasets that track AI's impact on labor, task automation, and economic adoption, here are the most comparable resources:
AI Labor Market Exposure (Task & Occupation Mappings)
 * OpenAI & UPenn "GPTs are GPTs" Dataset: Maps O*NET tasks to Large Language Model exposure, evaluating which jobs are most likely to be automated or augmented.
 * **Eloundou et al. (2023) Task-Level Exposure: Quantifies whether it is theoretically possible for an LLM to complete specific O*NET tasks at least twice as fast.
 * Felten, Raj, and Seamans' AI Occupational Exposure (AIOE): Links AI research progress to O*NET worker abilities to measure exposure across occupations, industries, and geographies.
Broad AI Economy & Labor Trends
 * Stanford HAI AI Index Report: An annual, comprehensive dataset tracking global AI corporate investment, labor market shifts, and economic impact.
 * Indeed Hiring Lab Data: Provides real-time labor market tracking, specifically focusing on the rise of job postings requiring AI skills and the exposure of different roles to Generative AI.
 * World Economic Forum (WEF) Future of Jobs Report: Offers macro-level datasets on global automation trends, expected job displacement, and cross-industry technology adoption.
Firm-Level AI Adoption
 * US Census Bureau (Annual Business Survey): Features official government data tracking the actual adoption and integration of AI technologies by businesses across various sectors.
 * Goldman Sachs AI Impact Data: Their major research reports include heavily cited data on automation potential and projected productivity gains across different industries.

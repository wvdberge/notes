Is IVA vs WGA a natural mapping of severely disabled applicant vs partially disabled applicant? Yes, it seems so by definition.

But can it therefore also help us cleanly distinguish L&P2015's health channel versus the skills channel?

The ultimate problem seems to be that in the real world, health & skills are correlated. You would need something that ONLY varies health, while leaving skills. Or something that ONLY varies skills, while leaving health. In L&P these are different stochastic processes.

We originally had the idea to use the prediction model to do exactly this: keep the health channel fixed, and the difference between the DiD on Pr(WIA | health) and the DiD on WIA would then be the opportunity costs part.
